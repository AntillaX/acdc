    How to play AC/DC

    The game

    Two players. Each turn one attacks and one defends. Roles swap whenever the defender successfully blocks.

    A turn

    Both players privately pick a number from 1 to 3. Picks reveal at the same time once both are in (or 15 seconds pass).

    • Numbers match → BLOCKED. No score. Turn ends, roles swap.
    • Numbers differ → attacker scores their pick (1, 2, or 3). Same roles, another pick round; the turn keeps going until somebody finally blocks.

    Pick timer

    15 seconds per pick. If both picks land sooner, it resolves immediately. Run out of time and the system picks for you: 1 if you're attacking, 3 if you're defending.

    Length

    5 attack turns each, 10 total — player A attacks turn 1, B attacks turn 2, A turn 3, and so on.

    Winning

    • 30-point lead at any moment ends the game instantly.
    • After 10 turns, whoever has more points wins.
    • Tied at the end of 10? Sudden death — alternating turns continue until somebody leads at the end of a turn.
